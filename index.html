The Gem Collector Game

You are a dealer of fine gems. Buy a package that matches the price your buyer is looking to spend in order to make a deal, but don't go over or you will lose out.

When the game starts, you will be given the buyer's offer. Click on the images of gems to add their hidden value to your inventory. Remember their values and keep buying until the value of your inventory matches the buyer's offer, resulting in a deal, and a win for you. Be careful of how much you are spending, however, because if you exceed the offer, the buyer will walk away from the deal, leaving you with a loss.

Win or lose, you can continue on to a new buyer each round, but when you do the buyer's offer and the prices for each type of crystal will change. Good luck, and happy gem dealing!
